Lily and Tom were twins who liked to decorate things. They had a big box of crayons, stickers, and glitter. One day, they found a shiny copper pot in the kitchen. It was Mom's pot, but she was not home. Lily and Tom wanted to make it more pretty. Suddenly, Mom appeared in the room. She was surprised to see the pot. Lily and Tom were so happy. They ran to Mom and hugged her. Mom smiled and said, "Oh, it's so beautiful! I'm so proud of you both." Lily and Tom smiled and hugged Mom back. They were so happy to have made Mom smile.
